### üß† Optimized Writing Workflow (Deep Work Friendly)

This workflow minimizes tool hopping and clarifies each step's role.

#### Step 0 ‚Äî Topic Framing (ChatGPT)
- Clarify idea, audience, structure
- Turn notes into outline with section goals
- Identify required assets (emails, Confluence, diagrams)
- **Output:** Clean outline in Markdown

#### Step 1 ‚Äî Research (Perplexity)
- Gather Confluence, emails, external references
- Summarize findings into structured notes
- **Output:** Research notes for drafting

#### Step 2 ‚Äî First Draft (Claude)
- Generate structured Markdown draft from outline and research
- Ensure logical flow and clear sectioning
- **Output:** First draft

#### Step 3 ‚Äî Structure & Synthesis (ChatGPT)
- Refactor for clarity and hierarchy
- Merge multiple sources
- Tighten language and remove fluff
- Add diagrams if needed (Mermaid / ASCII)
- **Output:** Refined draft

#### Step 4 ‚Äî Fact Verification (Gemini)
- Validate factual accuracy, dates, references
- **Output:** Verified draft

#### Step 5 ‚Äî Tone & Style Polish (Grok)
- Adjust tone for LinkedIn / SharePoint / internal audiences
- Smooth phrasing without altering structure
- **Output:** Final polished copy

#### Step 6 ‚Äî Platform Prep (Copilot)
- Convert Markdown to SharePoint News or LinkedIn format
- Insert diagrams and links
- **Output:** Publish-ready content

- Use ‚ÄúCurious Penguins Craft Great Golden Content‚Äù ‚Äî mnemonic matches tool order and is easy to visualize.

## Strict Style Guidelines ("Anti-AI Rules")

1. **Banned Words**
   Never use: *delve, unleash, unlock, elevate, leverage, realm, landscape, tapestry, testament, game-changer, in conclusion, summary, moreover, firstly, secondly.*

2. **Start in the middle**
   No definitions. No "In today's world."
   Open with a hard truth, concrete observation, or slightly uncomfortable fact.

3. **Rhythm matters**
   Mix short, blunt sentences with longer, reflective ones.
   Break rules when needed. Avoid robotic subject-verb-object repetition.

4. **Concrete over abstract**
   Never say "solutions exist."
   Name specific actions, behaviors, institutions, people, or visible outcomes.

5. **Clear stance**
   No hedging. No academic distancing.
   Speak with conviction and responsibility.

6. **Structure**
   * Use descriptive subheads (avoid "Introduction" or "Conclusion").
   * Do not summarize at the end.
   * End with a forward-looking line that lingers.

## Pre-Recording Checklist

### Production Quality Guidelines
- [ ] **Appearance**: Professional dress (Shave/Shower/Look good)
- [ ] **Connection**: Use Zoom 4K (NOT OCNA)
- [ ] **Environment**:
  - [ ] Good lighting (face clearly visible)
  - [ ] Clean/professional background
  - [ ] Quiet location (no background noise)
- [ ] **Distractions**: Close all notifications (Slack, Pages, Email) - "For 15 minutes, the world can wait"
- [ ] **Materials**: Prepare any demos/screen shares in advance
- [ ] **Audio**: Test microphone levels before recording
- [ ] **Props** (if applicable): Cup of tea/coffee for casual discussions

### Communication Guidelines
- [ ] Speak loudly and clearly with natural pauses (aids editing)
- [ ] Look at camera when speaking (creates connection)
- [ ] Enunciate clearly
- [ ] No profanity
- [ ] Keep energy high and engaging
